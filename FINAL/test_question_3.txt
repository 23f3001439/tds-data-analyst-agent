The Indian high court judgement dataset contains judgements from the Indian High Courts, downloaded from the ecourts website: https://judgments.ecourts.gov.in/. 

It contains judgments of 25 high courts, along with raw metadata (as .json) and structured metadata (as .parquet). The S3 path to the structured metadata is:

s3://indian-high-court-judgments/metadata/parquet/year=*/court=*/bench=*/metadata.parquet?s3_region=ap-south-1

Here are the columns available:

court_code, title, description, judge, pdf_link, cnr, date_of_registration, decision_date, disposal_nature, court, raw_html, bench, year
